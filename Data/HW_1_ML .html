here is the dataset link: Loan Eligiblity Dataset

General Overview :¶

  1. ### Problem Description: 
    - there are an significant need for many companies an banks that specialize  in providing home loans. 
- in this dataswet the company operate in diverse areas, including urban, semi-urban, and rural regions.
- When a customer applies for a home loan, the company needs to assess the customer's eligibility for the loan.
- the eligiblity for the loan based on various details provided in the online application form. 
- The customer(the applicant for the loan) details include:
      . Gender      . Marital              . Status
      . Education   . Number of Dependent  . Income
      . Loan Amount . Credit History       . Others
- the main purpose is To streamline and automate the loan eligibility process in real-time,
- The goal is to identify specific customer segments that are eligible for a loan amount.
- By doing so, the company aims to target these eligible customers more efficiently.
  1. ### About the dataset
    • this dataset contains information about loan eligibility
    • we can describe the dataset as folows :
    • it contains 614 rows
    • it contains 13 columns
    • the columns are as follows :
      1. Loan ID : Unique identifier for each loan.(its not important for our analysis ).
      2. Gender: Categorical variable indicating the gender of the loan applicant.
      3. Married: Categorical variable indicating the marital status of the loan applicant.
      4. Dependents: Numeric variable representing the number of dependents the loan applicant has.
      5. Education: Categorical variable indicating the educational level of the loan applicant.
      6. Self Employed: indicated whether the loan applicant is self-employed. (y/n)
      7. ApplicantIncome: Numeric variable representing the income of the loan applicant.
      8. Co applicant Income: Numeric variable representing the income of the co-applicant (if any).
      9. Loan Amount: Numeric variable representing the loan amount.
      10. Loan Amount Term: Numeric variable representing the term of the loan in months.
      11. Credit History: indicate whether the credit history of the applicant meets the guidelines.
      12. Property Area:indicate the area where the property is located. can be Urban, Semi-Urban ,Rural.
      13. Loan_Status: variable indicate whether the loan was approved. Y (approved) or N (not approved).
  2. ### Statistics and Results ##### after doing some statistics on the dataset we get the folowing reults 
         - the percentage of the rejected loans is about 30% 
     - the percentage of the accepted loans is about 70%
     - the mean income of the loan applicant is about 5403
     - there are some nan values in the loan amount and other feature 
     - we fix the nan value for the numerical fields by set it to the mean of the others values 
     - for the categorical fields we fill it using the mode method
